Most AI setups get built once and then go stale. You train a Custom GPT on your offer in January. You change your offer in March. Now it's writing copy for a product you don't sell anymore. So you go back in. Retrain it. Update the instructions. Six weeks later you're doing it again.

That's a static setup pretending to be a system.

The Living Workspace is one Claude workspace built around the current reality of your business. When something shifts, the workspace works from the updated version. You stop re-explaining your business every time you open a chat. You stop fighting AI that's operating from last quarter. You update one thing, and the system carries it forward.
